Margarita

Description

Basic margarita with fresh lime and optional Spicy and Cadillac variations.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz - fresh squeezed lime juice
  • 3 oz - Tequila
  • 1.5 oz - Triple Sec (Opt: Use Fresh Sqeezed Orange Juice)
  • For Cadillac: splash of Grand Marnier
  • Opt. Salt for Rim
  • Opt. Spicy: 1-5 Slices Jalepeno based on spicy level

Directions

  1. For Spicy, muddle Jalepno slices, add Tequila, and let sit for at least a few minutes while making the rest of hte drink
  2. Squeeze lime
  3. If salted rim use lime to wet rim and spin glass in salt
  4. Fill glass with ice.
  5. Add Tequila and strained lime juice.
  6. For Cadillac add splash of Grand Marnier.
