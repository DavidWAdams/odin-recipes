Wet 'P' Shots

Description

Great shot which is fruity and sweeet with very little alcohol flavor and will get you messed up fast.

Ingredients (makes 4 shots)

  • 2 oz - Tito's Vodka
  • 2 oz - Gin
  • 2 oz - Malibu Coconut Rum
  • 2 oz - Peach Schnapps
  • 6 oz - Cranberry Juice
  • 6 oz - Pineapple Juice

Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a shaker.
  2. Shake hard for 20 sec.
  3. Strain into 4 shot glasses.
