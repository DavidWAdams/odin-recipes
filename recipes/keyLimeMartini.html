Key Lime Martini

Description

A tart and tasty key lime drink.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz - Fresh squeezed key lime juice
  • 1.5 oz - Absolute Vanilla Vodka
  • 1 oz - Triple Sec (Opt: Use Fresh Sqeezed Orange Juice)

Directions

  1. Fill shaker with ice.
  2. Add alcohol and strained lime juice.
  3. Shake vigorously for 20 sec.
  4. Pour in martini glass
