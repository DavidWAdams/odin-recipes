Key Lime Pie

Description

This recipe starts with a graham cracker crust. Keebler Ready Crust works great! It as simple as mixing the pie filling, pouring into the crust, baking, cooling, making the frosting, putting on the frosting, chilling for 3 hours, then enjoy.

Ingredients

Pie Crust

  • 1 - Keebler Ready Made pie crust, 9 or 10 inch

Pie Filling

  • 28 oz sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup light sour cream
  • 3/4 cup key lime juice
  • zest from 2 regular limes or 4 key limes

Topping

  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 3/4 cup key lime juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

Key Lime Filling

  1. Preheat Oven to 375F.
  2. Grate zest from limes in a small plate
  3. Whisk together sweetened condensed milk, sour cream, lime juice, and lime zest in a medium bowl.
  4. Pour into prepared graham cracker crust.
  5. Bake for 10 minutes.
  6. Let pie cool slightly before chilling.
  7. Chill for 3 hours.
