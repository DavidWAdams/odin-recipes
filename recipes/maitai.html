Mai Tai

Description

Simple Mai Tai that will get you messed up fast.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz - Fresh squeezed lime juice
  • 1 oz - Bacardi Light Rum
  • 1 oz - Bacardi Dark Rum
  • 1 oz - Malibu Coconut Rum
  • 1 oz - Triple Sec (Opt: Use Fresh Sqeezed Orange Juice)
  • 6 oz - Fruit Punch (Opt: mix with pineapple juice)

Directions

  1. Fill tall glass with ice.
  2. Add Alcohol and strained lime juice.
  3. Stir
  4. Top of with Fruit Punch.
