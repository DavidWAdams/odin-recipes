Manhattan

Description

Delicious wiskey drink with a depth of flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz - Rye Whiskey
  • 1 oz - Sweet Vermouth
  • 2 dashes - Angostura bitters
  • 1 dash - Orange bitters
  • Garnish - Maraschino cherry
  • Garnish - Orange Peal

Directions

  1. Place a few large ice cubes or single whisky ice ball in a whiskey glass.
  2. Add Whiskey, Vermouth and bitters.
  3. Add a dash of Maraschino cherry juice.
  4. Garnsh with Maraschino cherry and orange peel.
